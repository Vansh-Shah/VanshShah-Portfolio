## — The Story

Six years leading teams under pressure. Two years keeping banking systems online for mutual banks and credit unions across Australia. Now building toward cybersecurity leadership — Master of Cybersecurity at UNSW, averaging 80%.

It didn't start in IT. Before any SQL query or incident ticket, I spent six years supervising the front end at Woolworths — managing teams, de-escalating difficult situations, and learning that calm under pressure is something you earn shift by shift. That foundation doesn't come from a textbook.

RMIT's Bachelor of Information Technology gave me the technical language to match those instincts, and opened the door into fintech. I joined Ultradata Australia in February 2024 and was promoted to Assistant Technical Support Manager seven months later.

The Master of Cybersecurity at UNSW is the next step — moving from responding to incidents to preventing them. The goal is senior security leadership: not just someone who can fix things, but someone who can build organisations that are resilient before things break.
